# Padho Label — User Guide

**Scan any packaged product. Know instantly whether it’s good for *your* body — and why.**
No account. No tracking. Everything stays on your phone.

---

## Set up your profile (1 minute)

On first launch, tell Padho about you — age, diet, goals (weight loss, muscle gain,
blood sugar…), and any conditions (diabetes, hypertension, PCOS, thyroid…). This is what
makes every score *yours*. It lives only on your device and you can edit it anytime from
the **Profile** tab.

> You can use Padho without a profile too — you’ll get the standard health grade instead
> of a personalised one.

---

## Scan a product

1. Tap the **Scan** button (centre of the bottom bar).
2. Point the camera at the **barcode**.
3. You’ll get a verdict in a second:
   - a **grade (A–E)** and a **0–100 Padho Score** personalised to you,
   - the **nutrition** breakdown with your personal daily-value %,
   - **ingredient & additive** flags (preservatives, colours, banned/▶ watch-list items),
   - plain-language **“For You”** notes tailored to your goals and conditions.

No barcode, or not in our data? Tap **Scan the label** and photograph the nutrition
panel — Padho reads the values for you, and you can correct anything before saving.

---

## Compare and decide (the smart part)

On any product, tap **⚖️ Compare healthier options**. Padho ranks the comparable
products it knows — *scored for your profile* — and leads with what matters to you:

- diabetic → **lowest sugar** first,
- hypertension → **lowest sodium**,
- muscle gain → **most protein**,
- weight loss → **fewest calories**.

The best pick is marked, the one you scanned is highlighted, and tapping any card opens
its full breakdown. The more you scan, the more options appear here — Padho learns your
shelf over time.

---

## Your pantry & history

- **History** keeps every scan so you can look things up later.
- **Pantry** tracks what you keep at home — add a product from its result screen.

Both are stored locally on your device.

---

## Why a product might say “not yet rated”

Indian packaged-food data is patchy, so occasionally a product isn’t in Padho’s catalog
yet. When that happens Padho **won’t guess** — it shows what it can read and offers to
**scan the label**. Once you do, that product is remembered, so it’s instant next time
(and it helps improve the catalog for everyone).

---

## Privacy

- **No account, no login.** Your profile, history, pantry, and favourites never leave
  your phone.
- Padho looks products up against [Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/)
  and a free label-reading service — it only ever sends a barcode or a label photo, never
  who you are or your health profile.

---

## A note on accuracy

Padho Label is an **informational tool, not medical advice**. Nutrition data is
community-sourced and may be incomplete or out of date — always check the pack and
consult a qualified professional for dietary decisions.
